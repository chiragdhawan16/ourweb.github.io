DIVYA PHARMA DISTRIBUTORS

Is one of the largest distributors in the state of Telangana.

The company has been founded by K. VIVEKANANDA and K. VENKATESHWARLU who were vastly experienced in pharmaceutical trade with over a decade of experience in the retail sector with this marked the humble beginning of Divya pharma in Telangana.

Today we are dealing with more than 22,000+ Products, 300+ pharmaceutical Multinational and Indian companies serving and 3000+ customers both retail and hospitals based pharmacies.

Under the tenure of K. VIVEKANANDA and K. VENKATESHWARLU, Divya pharma has grown immensely with the establishment of state of art dispatch and logistics. Making the company one of the largest distributors in the state of Telangana. From Day 1 of our business in 1995 till date after three decades passed. We still follow the same mantra.

Service in the Cause of Medicine and Humanity.

VISION

We purchase our products directly from the manufacturers and they are sold either to pharmacies or hospitals who are holding a valid drug license.

We ensure transparency and efficiency in our sales. Do not involve ourselves into sub-distribution activity.

The regular stock checks ensure that our supply operates at maximized efficiency.

MISSION

It is our goal and duty to provide high-quality products at the right price and right place to Build trust, Reduce reputational risk, Improve operational efficiency.

To provide outstanding service in distribution where stock shortage are never a problem especially in a time of need.

VALUES

Customer satisfaction is one of our core value. We believe if you don’t appreciate your customers, someone else will for us customer service means serve customers more than just solving problems or addressing complaints.

Divyapharma is a running family business which has been passed down from Generation to generation, Maintaining high standards and thereby preserving “Happy Customers”.
